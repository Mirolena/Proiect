ABOUT US

Our website started out of a coversation about how much the food in a country you visit can bring you closer to the people of that culture. In addition to that, food holds so many memories, and sometimes it can really take you "around the world".

That's how we decided to create this website, where you , and many foodies like you, can let the food bring you closer, no matter where you are.
